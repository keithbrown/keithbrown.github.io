About Me



I'm a software developer by day. I've been very fortunate to work with great people throughout my career, both co-workers and customers. I've worked with a wide array of technologies in both closed-source and open-source software.

I've been blessed in my job to also travel to Europe a few times. I've visited Hungary, Denmark, Sweden, and England.

At home I enjoy the outdoors. I'm involved in scouting organizations with both my sons and daughters. I've camped in numerous places in the Midwest. While backpacking I've done a 4 day section hike on the Appalachain Trail, a 5 day hike at Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore in the Michigan U.P. and shorter hikes in Indiana.

I enjoy fishing, mostly for largemouth bass, when I can make time.

I also enjoy reading and being an active parent in my kids' activities.
